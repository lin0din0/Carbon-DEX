What we built

One exchange. One registry. Live visibility.

CARBON is designed to move issuance, trading, and retirement into a single on-chain protocol. Every credit traces from mint to burn. Every trade settles publicly. Regulators and markets would read the same book. In real time, not 18 months later.

On-chain registry

One immutable record for every EUA. No duplicate national silos.

Direct settlement

Atomic company-to-company trades. No broker. No reconciliation lag.

Live transparency

Volume, prices, and inventory state visible continuously. Not on a PDF.

EU ETS native

Compliance rules encoded at the protocol boundary. Not bolted on top.

Background

Why the EU carbon market needs this

The EU ETS is the largest carbon market on earth. Designed in the early 2000s: national registries, bilateral brokers, annual cycles. A market clearing €3B a day with no single source of truth and no audit trail anyone can read in real time.
